## BUG/CORE: rough edges around text

Around text there are always rough edges of around ~1px blackness that are not the color of the text color itself.

Expect no edge. Ideally could even smoothen edges.

## BUG/CORE: rough edges in canvas with high `velocityDissipation`

When `velocityDissipation=1` is set to 1, rough edges appear very visible when 'splashing around'.
When `velocityDissipation=0.5` no edges show and they smoothen out.
